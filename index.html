What is Lambda Phi Epsilon

The World's Largest
Asian-Interest Fraternity

Lambda Phi Epsilon (ΛΦΕ) is the largest and only international Asian-interest fraternity, with over 10,000 brothers across the world united by a shared commitment to brotherhood, service, and cultural pride.

Founded on the belief that leadership and community go hand in hand, Lambda Phi Epsilon provides a space where Asian American men and allies can grow together — academically, personally, and professionally.

Our brothers represent the full spectrum of Asian American identity, building connections that last a lifetime while making a lasting impact on the communities around them.

Philanthropy

National Marrow Donor Program

Our national philanthropy is the National Marrow Donor Program (NMDP). We collaborate with NMDP to help individuals in need of bone marrow transplants.

Our fraternity actively raises awareness for leukemia and various blood disorders through local #BeTheMatch campaigns, registering committed donors to enhance the probability for patients to discover a life-saving match.

Our Chapter

The Beginning

In the Fall of 2019...

Our principal founders Laajlim Khang, Thang Nguyen, and Devin Vue dreamt of creating and fostering another inclusive space for Asian-American males and their nuanced identities. They went on to recruit ten more men and eventually formed the interest group HEARTH.

On October 25, 2020, the University of Minnesota Associate Chapter of Lambda Phi Epsilon was officially established as the 71st chapter of the organization.

We are dedicated to fostering an inclusive space at UMN-Twin Cities — involved in leadership, educational, and advocacy initiatives, creating a safe space for our Asian-American community to celebrate our culture and connections.

The First & Only International Asian-Interest Fraternity

Why Lambdas?

Our organization believes in the potential all people have in inflicting change within themselves and those around them. We welcome any men and non-binary people from any demographic to help them realize their potential in their connections and the community they foster.

Lambda Phi Epsilon offers not only a brotherhood within our chapter, but connections throughout the multicultural greek council and across the world. With 70+ chapters, we create a sense of community internationally that is only possible through our organization.

01

Community & Connections

Lambda Phi Epsilon allows our brothers to foster a sense of community within our fraternity, the greater multicultural Greek council, and organizations across the world.

02

Philanthropy & Advocacy

We aim to increase awareness for advocacy topics with a special focus on APIDA initiatives, creating inclusive spaces through collaboration and events.

03

Leadership & Development

Guided by our motto, “To Be Leaders Among Men,” our fraternity cultivates the next generation of leaders within the APIDA community and beyond.
